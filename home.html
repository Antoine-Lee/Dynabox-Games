About Us

Shadowbyte games strive to develop a range of diverse, immersive and accessible gameplay experiences, ranging from bean based first person shooters to chaotic, geometrical dimension defining games.

Shadowbyte games was first formed in the late months of 2019, when the developer Antoine first experimented with the intricacies between programming, character development and animation, and just like that; Shadowbyte’s first game, Splatterbeans. The company, from that point on, has only reached greater heights.

We have evolved to span several genres and our games serve diverse and faceted gamers, from the most experienced to first-timers. Shadowbyte Games was founded on the principles of ease of play, accessibility and, most importantly, entertainment, values we have instilled in each and every one of our games.

Our Games

Splatterbeans

Splatterbeans is a multiplayer first person shooter involving violent beans! Choose from a selection of weapons, and get ready to fight, try your best not to splat. "To bean, or not to bean"; that is the question.

Your Nemesis

The game takes place in the town of Ravenkeep, the fantasy world of your nemesis, two years after the events of the twenty year war between the armies of knights. Xalvador is investigating the invasion on Ravenkeep by a mysterious species, under the help of his mentor Randall. Through fighting this foreign species, he finds that not everything is as simple as it seemed at first, and that he has blindly followed pure observation into a trap.

Dimentional Distortion

Dimensional Distortion is a chaotic, multidimensional game, where you can cross between the different dimensions. Each dimension has a different set of rules, and it’s up to you to cross between them with precise timing, and accuracy. Dodge geometrical obstacles to the beat of the music, and dodge your way through to the end of each level. Not all the dimensions are the way you think, and there may be more than you expect.
